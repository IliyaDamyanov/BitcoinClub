Mission Bitcoin Club

  • Promotion and education in the field of cryptocurrencies with a main focus on Bitcoin
  • Creating a community of users, developers and enthusiasts of cryptocurrencies with a main focus on Bitcoin
  • Providing information and advice on the legal and regulatory framework of cryptocurrencies and Bitcoin
  • Uniting the interests of its members and supporting their development, qualification and knowledge
  • Stimulating dialogue and discussions, as well as creating an authoritative source of quality information
  • Uniting and attracting new members with similar interests
  • Promoting cooperation and contacts between members and the community
  • Supporting and stimulating the adoption of Bitcoin and other cryptocurrencies as a technological solution
